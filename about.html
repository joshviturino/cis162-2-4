The bath/sauna should be an offer where you and yours can have great experiences through a floating sauna. Here you can sit in a good and warm room, close to nature, regardless of weather and wind. In all seasons. What should Badet be? The bath is the first floating sauna in Østfold. The concept provides access to a floating sauna, cloakroom, sun deck and bathing ladder. The sauna room is intended for approx. 10-15 people. The sauna can be visited during drop-in time or booking. You can also rent the entire sauna privately. Everyone does. Almost, at least. Floating saunas have gradually become a popular concept in Norway. As in Finland, this has become a thing that everyone, at all ages thinks is fun and delicious. For many, it has become almost addictive. Floating saunas are definitely something that suits us Østfolders as well. As of today, there is a lack of an offer. Therefore, we envisage launching the sauna during the spring of 2022.
